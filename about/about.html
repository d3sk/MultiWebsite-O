Hockey Central was founded and created in 2018 by Olivia Barton. It started as a small online store, but now we have sold over 7,000 products throughout Australia. We sell a wide range of brands but mainly Bauer due to high demand. You can find all your ice hockey needs including sticks, protective gear and skates.

Feel free to follow us on our social media platforms and keep updated with news and sales.
